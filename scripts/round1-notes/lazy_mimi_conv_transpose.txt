## Notes
Sub-port 2 of port-mimi-conv.md: ports StreamableConvTranspose1d from fuel-transformers/src/models/audio/mimi/conv.rs to the lazy-graph API as fuel-core/src/lazy_mimi_conv_transpose.rs. Reuses bake_weight_norm helper from lazy_mimi_conv (the in-axis-first WN math is identical — only the outer dim name changes). Adds StreamableConvTranspose1dWeights::{new, forward, step} plus value-passed StreamConvTranspose1dState carrying the trailing (kernel - stride) output samples between chunks. One-shot forward applies conv_transpose1d (via the existing rank-3 LazyTensor primitive), adds bias as a broadcast_add of a (1, Cout, 1)-reshaped const, then trims kernel-stride from the right (causal) or splits between the two ends (non-causal). Streaming step runs the raw transposed conv per chunk, subtracts bias from prev_ys (to avoid double-counting it on the overlap region the new chunk also biased), sums the new chunk's head with prev_ys, concats the new chunk's tail, and splits the result at ot - (kernel - stride) — first part emits, second part becomes the new prev_ys. Nine tests cover the WN-baking shape contract, one-shot shape + finite + hand-computed numerics, and chunk-by-1/2/larger streaming-equivalence against one-shot across kernel/stride/bias combos. Test infrastructure detail: two missing sibling-project paths (../aocl and ../vulkane referenced by workspace Cargo.toml) had to be junctioned into .claude/worktrees/ so the workspace could load its members — those junctions are filesystem-only and don't change any committed file.
