## Notes
Single-file lazy port of fuel-transformers Gemma4 audio encoder (Conformer).

Design choices vs eager:
- No `audio_mel_mask` input — v1 assumes uniformly padded mel. The eager mask logic (validity mask × causal mask) is deferred to a follow-up batching session.
- Chunked attention is implemented via a full (T, T) block-band additive mask built host-side and broadcast-added to QKᵀ scores, rather than the eager's `extract_block_context` 5-D matmul. Cleaner lazy graph at equivalent semantics for v1's no-mask single-input path.
- Relative position bias uses the spec's Shaw-style learnable `(2*max_rel + 1, num_heads)` table + index_select, NOT the eager's sinusoidal-projection + relative-shift trick. The spec explicitly described the Shaw form.

Test surface:
- `forward_shape_and_finite_tiny`: tiny config (model_dim=16, depth=2, num_heads=4, chunk_size=16, left_chunks=1, mel_T=64, n_mels=8). Sanity-checks output shape `(1, 16, 16)` (T 64→32→16 after two stride-2 SSCP convs) and all-finite output. F_out 8→4→2 inside SSCP; flattened C_out·F_out=32 projected to hidden_size=16.
- `chunked_attention_mask_block_band`: 4 chunks of 16 frames + left_chunks=1; mask is host-built via the public `chunked_band_mask_values` and asserted cell-by-cell — attendable cells are 0.0, blocked cells are < -1e8.
- `rel_pos_bias_table_lookup`: hand-traces (i,j)→bucket for tiny config, validates diagonal at max_rel, validates +3/-3 offsets, then realizes the model's `build_rel_pos_indices` U32 tensor and compares vector-equal.
