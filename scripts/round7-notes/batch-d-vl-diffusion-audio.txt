All 10 binaries hit the same root infrastructure gap: the shipped lazy_* modules for vision-language and diffusion models expose tightly-typed weight structs (e.g. ClipModelWeights, PaligemmaWeights, FluxWeights) but do NOT ship MmapedSafetensors-based loaders. The eager binaries are 1-to-1 weight-loader-driven (`VarBuilder::from_mmaped_safetensors` + `Model::new(vb, cfg)`), so without a loader the migration cannot preserve the binary's user-visible behavior (download weights from HF Hub → forward).

Worth noting that fuel-core's `load_tensor_as_f32` and `load_transposed_matrix` helpers are `pub(crate)`, so even writing inline per-binary loaders requires reimplementing those helpers in main.rs — making per-binary effort ~250-500 LOC each.
